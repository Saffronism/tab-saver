Privacy Policy for Tab Saver

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

Tab Saver is a browser extension that helps users save and organize their open browser tabs. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Tab Saver collects the following information:

  • Tab Information: URLs, titles, and favicons of open browser tabs that you choose to save

How We Use Your Data

The tab information you save is used solely to:

  • Display your saved tab sessions in the extension popup
  • Restore tabs when you click on them
  • Organize tabs into categories for easier management

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Transmit your data to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage or browsing behavior
  • Sell or monetize your data in any way

Permissions

Tab Saver requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: To access open tab information when saving
  • storage: To save tab data locally in your browser
  • activeTab: To identify the current active tab
  • host permissions: To restore tabs to any URL you previously visited

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support.
